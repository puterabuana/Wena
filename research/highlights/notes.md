## FAQ

- Wena uses a self-photo concept: customers take photos themselves without a photographer, using a remote shutter.
- Booking beforehand is recommended to avoid a long queue.
- Included: free all softcopy, 2 photo strips, and 15 minutes of photo session.
- Extra time: IDR 10K per extra 5 minutes, depending on customer schedule availability.
- Props available: glasses, flowers, dolls, headbands, costumes, and more.
- Customers may bring their own props such as cake, decoration, balloons, flowers, or other props.
- Children under 2 years old are free.
- Changing clothes is allowed; Wena provides a changing area, but the timer continues during clothing changes.
- Shoes/sandals are allowed as long as they are clean and dry before entering the studio from the first floor.
